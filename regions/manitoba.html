📋 Manitoba-Specific Info

🛡️ MPI (Manitoba Public Insurance)

Basic Coverage

Mandatory Coverage Includes:

  • $200,000 liability minimum
  • Personal injury protection (no-fault)
  • $500,000 third-party liability standard
  • Accident benefits

Extension Coverage

  • Comprehensive & collision
  • Increased liability ($1M, $2M, $5M)
  • Rental vehicle coverage
  • Loss of use coverage

Driver Safety Rating (DSR)

Merit system from -20 to +15. Higher rating = lower premiums. Clean driving improves your DSR.

MPI Locations

Main office in Winnipeg, plus brokers across Manitoba for claims and services.

❄️ Winter Tire Requirements

❄️ Status: HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

Winter tires not mandatory in Manitoba, but essential for -30°C to -40°C winters

Insurance Benefit: Winter tire discount available through MPI extension coverage

🚗 Vehicle Registration & Licensing

New Residents

90 days to register vehicle and obtain Manitoba driver's license

Safety Inspection

Out-of-province vehicles require inspection before registration

Registration Renewal

Annual renewal on your birthday. Can be done online or at Autopac brokers

Sales Tax

7% PST + 5% GST = 12% total tax on vehicle purchases

🚦 Manitoba Driving Regulations

Speed Limits

  • Highway: 100 km/h
  • Urban: 50 km/h (unless posted)
  • School zones: 30 km/h when flashing
  • Playground zones: 30 km/h

Block Heaters

Essential for Manitoba winters. Many parking lots offer plug-ins. Block heater timers recommended.

Graduated Licensing

Class 5I (learner), Class 5I (intermediate), Class 5F (full). Zero alcohol tolerance for new drivers.

Distracted Driving

$672 fine + 5 demerits for handheld device use while driving
