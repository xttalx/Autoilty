🚦 BC Driving & Regulations

❄️ Winter Tires

Mandatory on most highways Oct 1 - Mar 31

3-peak mountain snowflake symbol required

Chains may be required in severe conditions

🔍 Emissions Testing (AirCare)

Applies to some regions

Lower Mainland & Fraser Valley regions

Every 2 years for vehicles 1992+

🚗 ICBC Insurance

Government-run auto insurance

No-fault Enhanced Care coverage

Optional coverage from private insurers

🚦 Right Turn on Red

Permitted unless signed

Must come to complete stop

Yield to pedestrians and traffic

📱 Distracted Driving

$368 fine + 4 points

Hands-free only

L/N drivers: no electronic devices

🚙 N Driver Restrictions

One passenger max (family exempt)

Zero alcohol tolerance

Display "N" sign
