🚦 Alberta Driving & Regulations

❄️ Winter Tires

Not mandatory but highly recommended

Many insurance discounts available

Essential for Calgary/Edmonton winters

🔍 Vehicle Inspections

Not required for registration renewal

Only for vehicles from out-of-province

Or vehicles over 12 years old (first registration)

🚗 Vehicle Registration

Annual renewal through registry agent

Private registry system (not government-run)

Online renewal available

🚦 Right Turn on Red

Permitted unless signed

Must come to complete stop

Yield to pedestrians and traffic

📱 Distracted Driving

$300 fine + 3 demerit points

Hands-free devices only

Applies to all electronic devices

💨 No Emissions Testing

Alberta has no emissions testing

No inspection required for air quality

One of few provinces without this requirement

🚗 Alberta Car Culture

🛻 Truck Country

Highest truck ownership in Canada

F-150, Silverado, Ram dominance

Diesel pickups very popular

❄️ Extreme Winter

-40°C temperatures common

Block heaters essential

Remote starters standard

🏔️ Mountain Access

Easy access to Rockies

4x4/AWD very popular

Weekend mountain trips common

💰 High Incomes

Strong economy = nice vehicles

Luxury trucks common

Performance cars popular

❄️ Alberta Winter Vehicle Prep

🔌

Block Heater Essential

Must-have for -30°C+ winters. Plug in when temperature drops below -15°C.

🔋

Battery Health Critical

Cold weather kills weak batteries. Test before winter, replace if over 4 years old.

🛞

Quality Winter Tires

Budget tires don't cut it. Invest in Blizzak, X-Ice, or Hakkapeliitta.

🚗

Remote Starter

Not a luxury in Alberta. Pre-warm your vehicle before driving.
