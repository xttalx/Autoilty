🚦 Ontario Driving & Regulations

❄️ Winter Tires

Not mandatory but highly recommended

Many insurance companies offer discounts for winter tire use

🔍 DriveClean (Emissions)

Currently suspended as of April 2019

No emissions testing required for light-duty vehicles

🚗 Right Turn on Red

Permitted unless signed otherwise

Come to complete stop and yield to pedestrians

📱 Distracted Driving

$615 fine + 3 demerit points

Hands-free devices only while driving
