📋 Saskatchewan-Specific Info

🛡️ SGI (Saskatchewan Government Insurance)

Basic Auto Insurance

Mandatory Coverage:

  • Liability: $200,000 minimum
  • No-fault injury coverage
  • Property damage coverage
  • Uninsured motorist coverage

Optional Coverage

  • Collision & comprehensive
  • Increased liability limits
  • Rental vehicle coverage
  • Road assistance

Safe Driver Recognition

Up to 20% discount for safe driving history. Star rating system rewards accident-free drivers.

SGI Offices

Regina headquarters, Saskatoon, Moose Jaw, Prince Albert, Swift Current, and other locations across SK.

❄️ Winter Tire Requirements

❄️ Status: HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

Winter tires not mandatory, but essential for Saskatchewan winters (-30°C to -40°C common)

Insurance Benefit: SGI offers premium reductions for winter tire use

🚗 Vehicle Registration & Licensing

New Residents

90 days to register vehicle and get SK driver's license

Vehicle Inspection

Out-of-province vehicles require SGI inspection before registration

Renewal

Annual registration renewal. Can be done online, by mail, or at motor license issuer

PST

6% PST + 5% GST = 11% total tax on vehicle purchases
