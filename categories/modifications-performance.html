⚠️ Before You Modify

🔒 Warranty Considerations

Performance mods may void your warranty. Check with dealer before modifying new vehicles.

🚔 Legal Requirements

Ensure modifications comply with provincial regulations. Some mods require inspections or certifications.

💰 Insurance Impact

Notify your insurance about major modifications. Failure to disclose may void coverage.

🔧 Professional Installation

Complex mods should be done by certified shops. DIY mistakes can be costly.
