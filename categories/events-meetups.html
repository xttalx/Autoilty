📋 Event Checklist

  • Choose date & venue
  • Get permits if needed
  • Create event thread
  • Promote on social media
  • Set RSVP deadline

📢 Promote Your Event

Post in regional forums, share on social media, and contact local car clubs. Early promotion ensures better turnout!

⚠️ Safety & Insurance

Ensure proper insurance coverage for organized events. Brief participants on safety rules and venue regulations.

📸 Share Photos

Post event photos and videos in the forum. Tag participants and thank sponsors. Build excitement for next time!
