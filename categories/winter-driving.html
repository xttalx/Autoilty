✅ Essential Winter Preparation Checklist

🛞 Tires & Wheels

  • ✓ Install winter tires (3-peak mountain snowflake)
  • ✓ Check tire pressure (drops in cold weather)
  • ✓ Inspect tread depth (minimum 4/32")
  • ✓ Consider dedicated winter wheel set

🔋 Battery & Electrical

  • ✓ Test battery (free at most shops)
  • ✓ Clean battery terminals
  • ✓ Check alternator charging
  • ✓ Test block heater if equipped

💧 Fluids

  • ✓ Antifreeze concentration (-40°C minimum)
  • ✓ Winter windshield washer fluid
  • ✓ Engine oil (consider 0W or 5W winter grade)
  • ✓ Check power steering and brake fluid

🚗 Emergency Kit

  • ✓ Ice scraper & snow brush
  • ✓ Jumper cables or booster pack
  • ✓ Blanket & warm clothes
  • ✓ Sand/salt & small shovel
  • ✓ Flashlight & first aid kit

📋 Provincial Winter Tire Requirements

🇫🇷 Quebec

MANDATORY December 1 - March 15

3-peak mountain snowflake symbol required

Fine: $200-$300

🌲 British Columbia

MANDATORY on highways October 1 - March 31

Mountain Snowflake (M+S minimum some routes)

Fine: $121

🏙️ Ontario

Recommended but not mandatory

Insurance discounts available

Essential for safety

⛰️ Alberta

Not mandatory

Highly recommended for winter conditions

Insurance discounts available
