Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

We collect information you provide directly to us, including when you create an account, post content, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, and any content you post on our platform.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
